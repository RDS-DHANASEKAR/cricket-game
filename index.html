How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Choose to play a Quick Match or Full Tournament
  • Select your team and opponent (for Quick Match)
  • Decide the number of overs (1-20)
  • Participate in the toss by choosing odd/even and entering a number
  • If you win the toss, choose to bat or bowl first
  • During the match, select a run (1-6) when it's your turn
  • If your number matches the computer's, it's a wicket!
  • In a tournament, the top 4 teams advance to playoffs

Controls:

  • Use mouse to click buttons
  • Number keys 1-6 can be used during match play

About Cricket Tournament Game

Welcome to Cricket Tournament - the ultimate virtual cricket experience!

This game was developed by cricket enthusiasts to bring the excitement of IPL to your browser.

Features:

  • Play quick matches or full tournaments
  • 10 authentic teams with logos and colors
  • Toss simulation with odd/even logic
  • Full tournament with playoffs system
  • Interactive match gameplay

Game Rules

Basic Rules

  • Each match consists of two innings (batting and bowling)
  • You can choose to play 1-20 overs per innings
  • Win the toss to decide whether to bat or bowl first
  • When batting, select runs from 1-6
  • If your selected number matches the computer's, you're out!

Tournament Rules

  • Round-robin league stage where all teams play each other
  • Top 4 teams qualify for playoffs
  • Playoffs follow IPL format: Qualifiers and Eliminator
  • 2 points for a win, 0 for a loss

Controls and Shortcuts

Mouse Controls

  • Click buttons to navigate menus
  • Click run buttons (1-6) during match play
  • Click toss options during the toss sequence

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • 1-6 keys: Select runs during match play
  • Enter: Confirm selections
  • Escape: Back to previous screen
